Turkish Dinner

Coming Together with the family

Turkish Dinner is the Coming together of the whole family to share a meal together. It usually starts with mezze and the main course is mostla a grilled meat dish.

Dishes and drinks at Turkish dinner

  • Adana kebap

  • Döner Kebap

  • Manti

  • Sac Tava

  • Köfte

  • Raki
