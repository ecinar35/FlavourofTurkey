Turkish meals

Turkish Breakfast

Turkish breakfast or kahvalti in turkish is a essential part of turkish culture. Turkish families will often get together at one table on weekends to have breakfast. The concept of sharing a meal is one of the main cultural aspects of turkish culture. Turkish culture emphasizes the importance of hospitality and generosity.
